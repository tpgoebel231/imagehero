Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

1. Zero-Upload Image Processing

At ImageHero.app, we believe your data belongs to you. Unlike traditional online photo editors, our tools utilize the HTML5 Canvas API. This means that when you use our Clone Stamp or Transparency tools, your images are processed locally in your browser's memory. Your files are never uploaded to our servers.

2. Information We Collect

Because all image processing happens on your device, we do not collect, store, or have access to any images you edit. We do not require account registration or collect personally identifiable information (PII) to use our tools.

3. Cookies and Third-Party Advertising

To keep our tools free for everyone, we use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site based on your visits to this and other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

4. Log Data

Like most websites, we collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our site ("Log Data"). This may include information such as your computer's IP address, browser type, and the pages you visit. This is used solely for analytics to improve the user experience.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the TPG Media team.
